Do you work different body parts on different days?

Yes, I rotate between Chest and Tris, Back and Bis, and Glutes and Legs. I usually work my abdominals daily, as they recover faster than the other major muscles groups. You want to give the groups a day or two to rest and recover in between workouts.

Do I need supplements?

Absolutely NOT! You can get all your nutrients needed from food. I utilize supplments with the busy lifestlye I have. If I know I won't be able to get enough Greens or Protein in my day, I'll utilize supplements to meet my daily cap.

I am a female, will lifting weights make me look manly?

No, hitting the weightroom will not make you look manly, and can actually help aid you in your weightloss journey.
