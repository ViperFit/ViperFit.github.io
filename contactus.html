Q = Do you work different body parts on different days?

A = Yes, I rotate between Chest and Tris, Back and Bis, Shoulders, and Glutes and Legs. I usually work my abdominals in some way daily, as they recover faster than the other major muscles groups. You want to give the groups a day or two to rest and recover in between workouts.

Q = Do I need supplements?

A = Absolutely NOT! You can get all your nutrients needed from food. I utilize supplements with the busy lifestlye I have. If I know I won't be able to get enough greens or protein in my day, I'll utilize supplements to meet my daily cap.
