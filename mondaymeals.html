BREAKFAST

Ingredients: 4 Eggs, 1 Lemon, Tsp Salt, Tsp Cayenne Pepper, 1/4 Cup Butter, 1 English Muffin, Romaine Lettuce, 1 Tomato, 2 Slices Bacon, 2 Tbs Apple Cider Vinegar

Directions: 1. Bring to a boil, 2 cups of water and apple cider vinegar. 2. Cook bacon in the oven, 410 degrees F for 10-15 mins (depending on your desired crispness). 3. Crack one egg into a bowl and stir the boiling water/vinegar mix. As the water is rotating in a circular motion drop your egg in and continue to swirl the water with your spoon gently to ensure you don't break the yolk. Cook the egg for about 3 minutes. 4. While the egg is cooking, toast your english muffin. 5. Once toasted dress each half of the muffin with lettuce, a slice of tomato, and 1 slice of bacon. 6. Take your cooked egg out, and gently set it on top of one of the muffin sides. Repeat step 3 with the other egg, and place it on top of the other slice once done. 7. Separate the two yolks from the last two eggs and toss the egg whites as we won't need them. 8. Mix 1/4 lemon juice, cayenne pepper, and salt to the yolks. 9. Melt the butter. Once done stir the butter into the yolk mix. Once blended well, heat the mixture in the microwave for 15-20 seconds and stir well again. 10. Last, drizzle the designed amount of hollandaise sauce on top of your Eggs Benedict. Garnish with salt, pepper, and green onions.



LUNCH

Ingredients: 1 Chicken Breast, 1 Honeycrisp Apple, 1/4 Red Onion, 1 Slice Bacon, Romaine, 1/8 Cup Feta, 1/4 Cup Dried Cranberries, 1/4 Cup Walnuts, 1/2 Cup Olive Oil, 1/2 Cup Honey, 1/2 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar, 1/4 Cup Dijon, 1 Tsp Salt, 1 Tsp Pepper

Directions: 1. First, we will start by making the dressing. Mix the olive oil, honey, apple cider vinegar, dijon, salt, and pepper into a bowl. Whisk until well blended, and taste test. Once, it is to your liking set it in fridge to cool. 2. Clean and butterfly chicken breast. Season it to your liking and grill. 3. While your chicken is grilling, pan fry your strip of bacon. 4. Pull the cooked chicken from the grill and your cooked bacon out of the pan. Set them both aside to cool. 5. Chop as much romaine as you'd like for your salad, and arrange on your plate. 6. Next, dice your apples and sprinkle over the bed of lettuce. 7. You can choose to either dice or slice the red onion, it really depends on your preference. Once done, sprinkle on top of lettuce and apples. 8. From there, you want to grab your feta, dried cranberries, and walnuts. Sprinkle them on top of your salad as well. 8. Grab your chicken and slice it into thin strips. Place the strips onto the salad. 9. Crumble your cooked bacon and sprinkle onto the salad. 10. Grab your dressing and drizzle about 1/4 cup all over your salad.




DINNER

Ingredients: 1 Pint Whipping Cream, 6 Shrimp, 1/4 Cup Shredded Parmesan, 1 Serving Fetticine, 1/2 Cup Panko, 1/2 Cup Coconut Flakes, 1 Tbs Butter, 1 Egg, 1/4 Cup Flour, 1 Tsp Salt, 1 Tsp Pepper, 1 Tbs Creole Seasoning, Pinch of Parsley, 1 Cup Vegetable Oil

Directions: 1. Bring 3 cups of water to to a boil, then toss in fetticine to cook. 2. Clean and devein the shrimp, and butterfly 2 of them. 3. Melt your butter into a pan, toss in the shrimp, and a pinch of your creole seasonings. Cook until pink and set aside. 4. Mix your panko and coconut into a bowl together, and whisk the eggs in a separate bowl. 5. Dredge your butterfly shrimp into the flour, then the eggs, and finally coat in the panko/coconut mixture. Place them into the fridge to cool, until ready to fry. 6. Remove pasta from stove, drain, and set aside. Heat the oil to fry the coconut shrimp. 7. Add whipping cream, salt, pepper, and creole into a saucepan and bring to boil. 8. Drop your shrimp into the hot oil to cook until golden brown, once done set them aside. 9. Add your other shrimp and fetticine to the sauce and continue to cook, sauce will thicken. 10. Place shredded parmesan in a circle on parchment paper, and set under broiler to get golden brown (this will be your garnish) 11. Place fetticine on plate and top with coconut shrimp, parmesan crisp, and parsley.
