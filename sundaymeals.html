BREAKFAST

Ingredients: 2 Slices Of Bacon, 3 Eggs, 1 Piece of Bread, 1/4 Onion, 4 Mushrooms, 1/4 tomato, 2 Tbs Butter, 1/4 Cup Shredded Cheese, 1 Tsp salt, 1 Tsp pepper, 2 Tbs Peanut Butter, 1 Banana

Directions: 1. Place bacon into oven at 410 degrees F for 10-12 mins, time can vary depending on your desired crispness. 2. Wash and dice mushrooms, tomato, and onion. 3. Melt 1 Tbs butter in a pan and sautee mushrooms and onions. Once cooked to your liking, remove from heat and set aside. 4. Pull bacon out of the oven and set aside to cool. 5. Whisk the eggs into a bowl, and add salt and pepper to season. 6. Melt the last of the butter into a frying pan, ensuring to coat the sides as well. 7. Pour your egg mixture into the pan. Spread it around with your spatula ensuring it is cooked through. 8. Sprinkle your cheese, mushrooms, onions, tomatoes, and bacon onto the eggs and allow the cheese to melt. 9. Toast your bread. Once done, place onto a plate and spread on the peanut butter. 10. Fold your omelet in half, and carefully place onto the plate. 11. Finally, slice your banana and place onto the peanut butter toast and enjoy. Keep in mind, there are several different ingredients you can place inside an omelet so don't be afraid to get creative.



LUNCH

Ingredients: 1 Chicken Breast, 1 Flour Tortilla, 1 Celery Stick, 1/4 Cup Almonds, 2 Tbs Dried Cranberries, 1/4 Cup Mayo, 1 Tbs Apple Cider Vinegar, 1/4 Tsp Salt, 1/4 Tsp Pepper

Directions: 1. Clean and butterfly the chicken breast. Season with salt and pepper, and grill. Once done, set the chicken breast aside to cool before cutting it into cubes. 2. Clean you celery stick and finely chop it. 3. Place the celery, dried cranberries, walnuts, mayo, apply cider vinegar, and a pinch of salt and pepper into a bowl mixing all the ingredients well. Place into the fridge to cool. 3. While your chicken cools cut your tortilla into triangle and bake in the over for 350 degrees F. Check them every few minutes and flip. Cooking them until the desired crispness. 4. Cut your grilled chicken into cubes, and place into the bowl with the other ingredients. 5. Set back into the fridge for about 15 mins to cool entirely. 6. Take your tortillas out of the oven and sprinkle with a pinch of salt. 7. Serve the chicken salad with the homemade tortilla chips. You can substitute pecans for the walnuts, and red grapes for the dried cranberries if you so choose. You can also choose to omit the celery if wanted.



DINNER

Ingredients: Ground Beef, 1/4 Red Onion, 2 Banana Peppers, 2 Tbs Feta, 1 Hamburger Bun, 1 Tbs Ketchup, 1 Tbs Mayo, 1/4 Tsp Salt, 1/4 Tsp Pepper, 1/4 Tsp Garlic, 1/4 Tsp Creole Seasoning, 1/4 Tsp Parsley, 1/2 Cup Curly Fries, 1 Tsp Butter

Directions: 1. Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, garlic, and creole seasonings. Form into patties and set aside until ready to grill. 2. Mix the mayo with the ketchup to create an aioli, place into a ziploc baggie, and set into the fridge for later use. 3. Thinly slice the red onions and banana peppers and set aside for later. 4. Grill or pan fry your burgers to your desired doneness. I prefer to have mine grilled, and is the method I chose for this recipe. 4. While your burger is on the grill. Place your curly fries into the oven at 375 degrees F for 15-20 mins, or until you've reached the desired crispness. 5. Melt the butter into a frying pan and toast your bun until golden brown. 6. Pull your burger from the grill and place onto one side of your toasted bun. Top with feta, banana peppers, and red onions. 7. Place the curly fries onto the plate. 8. Cut one corner of the ziploc baggie and drizzle the aioli onto the fries and other side of your bun. Finally, garnish your curly fries with parsley and enjoy.
