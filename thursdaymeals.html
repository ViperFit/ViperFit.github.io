BREAKFAST

Ingredients: 2 Slice of Bread, 2 Egg Whites, 2 Slices of Bacon, 1 Slice of American Cheese, 1 Honeycrisp Apple, 1 Tsp Butter

Directions: 1. Cook the bacon in the oven at 410 degrees F for 10-12 mins, depending on your desired crispness. Once done, remove from the oven and set aside for a later step. 2. Separate the eggs whites into a bowl, and toss the yolks away. 3. Melt the butter in a frying pan, ensuring to coat the sides. 4. Place your egg whites in the pan and spread them out with your spatula, ensuring they get cooked through. 5. While your eggs are cooking, go ahead and toast your bread. 6. Add the sliced cheese to your eggs and fold into a square, about the size of your bread. 7. Construct your sandwhich with your toast, cheesy eggs, and bacon. 8. Make an apple garnish. To do so slice one side of the apple. Lay the flat side down onto a cutting board, and make thing slices. Ensure you keep the slices together while cutting. 9. Stick a toothpick into one side of all the slices, and fan out. Cut a cube out of the remaining apple to act as your base, sticking the exposed toothpick into the cube. This will support your fanned apple.




LUNCH

Ingredients: 1 Chicken Breast, 1 Honeycrisp Apple, 1/4 Red Onion, 1 Slice Bacon, Spring Mix Lettuce, 1/8 Cup Feta, 1/4 Cup Dried Cranberries, 1/4 Cup Walnuts, 1/2 Cup Olive Oil, 1/2 Cup Honey, 1/2 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar, 1/4 Cup Dijon, 1 Tsp Salt, 1 Tsp Pepper

Directions: 1. First, we will start by making the dressing. Mix the olive oil, honey, apple cider vinegar, dijon, salt, and pepper into a bowl. Whisk until well blended, and taste test. Once, it is to your liking set it in fridge to cool. 2. Clean and butterfly chicken breast. Season it to your liking and grill. 3. While your chicken is grilling, pan fry your strip of bacon. 4. Pull the cooked chicken from the grill and your cooked bacon out of the pan. Set them both aside to cool. 5. Chop as much romaine as you'd like for your salad, and arrange on your plate. 6. Next, dice your apples and sprinkle over the bed of lettuce. 7. You can choose to either dice or slice the red onion, it really depends on your preference. Once done, sprinkle on top of lettuce and apples. 8. From there, you want to grab your feta, dried cranberries, and walnuts. Sprinkle them on top of your salad as well. 8. Grab your chicken and slice it into thin strips. Place the strips onto the salad. 9. Crumble your cooked bacon and sprinkle onto the salad. 10. Grab your dressing and drizzle about 1/4 cup all over your salad.




DINNER

Ingredients: 1 Chicken Breasts, 4 Mushrooms, 1 Carrot, 1/2 Cup Panko, 1 Egg, 1/4 Cup Flour, 1 Cup Vegetable Oil, 1 Tbs Green Onions, 1/2 Cup Broccoli, 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce, 1 Tbs Ginger, 1 Tbs Brown Sugar, 1/2 Cup Rice, 1/4 Tsp Salt, 1/4 Tsp Pepper

Directions: 1. Clean and thinly slice the chicken breast, and season with salt and pepper. 2. Whisk the egg in a bowl. 3. Clean your vegetables. Peel and slice the carrot in half. Slice one half, and peel the other half for garnish. 4. Slice your broccoli, green onions, and mushrooms and set aside for later use. 5. Heat oil. Dredge chicken into the flour, eggs, and finally the panko. Set aside until oil is hot enough. 6. Cook rice according to package instructions. Microwaveable rice is perfectly ok to utilize. 7. Heat a Tsp of oil in a pan and toss in the mushrooms and carrots with a pinch of salt and pepper. Sautee the vegetables until tender. Add the broccoli last just to get it seasoned with the veggies. You don't want to overcook your broccoli. Remove your vegetables from the pan and set aside 8. Place you chicken into the hot oil and fry until golden brown, and has an internal temp of 165 degrees F. 9. In the pan you used for the veggies, add your soy sauce, ginger, brown sugar. Cook until the sugar dissolves. 10. Finally, plate your rice first and layer your veggie mixture on top. Then, drizzle on your sauce and top it with your chicken. Finally, garnish with your green onions and carrot peels.
