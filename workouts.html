Daily Workouts



Monday (Back & Biceps)

Workout = Pull-Ups, Bent-Over Barbell Rows, Straight Arm Lat Pulldown, Seated Cable Row (Neutral Grip), Preacher Curls, Alternating Dumbbell Curls

Warm-Up = 20 Mins HIIT! Weights = 3 Rounds, 10-12 Sets! Equipment Needed = Dumbbells, Barbells, Pull-Up Bar. Resistance bands can be utilized to moderate Lat Pulldowns and Cable Rows.
